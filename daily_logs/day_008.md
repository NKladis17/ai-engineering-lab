## Learned
- Models read tokens (numbered chunks), not letters or words
- "strawberry" = ['str','aw','berry'] → why AI can't count letters
- Casing, spaces, numbers, emoji all tokenize in surprising ways
- Tokenization explains arithmetic weakness, casing sensitivity, token costs

## Evidence
- "antidisestablishmentarianism" = 6 tokens; 🍓 = 3 tokens; "Hello"≠"hello" by ID
