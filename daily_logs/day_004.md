# Day 4 — Cost & Latency Tracking

## Built
- cost_latency_logger.py: computes $ cost per call from token counts
- run_batch.py: runs 20 prompts automatically
- CSV log (cost_latency.csv) with 21 calls

## Learned
- Haiku 4.5 pricing: $1/M input, $5/M output (output is 5x)
- Cost formula: (input_tokens × input_price) + (output_tokens × output_price)
- Output length dominates BOTH cost and latency
- Call 3 ("one sentence") cost 11x less by producing fewer output tokens
- Most calls hit max_tokens=300 → paying for the cap every time

## Evidence
- Typical call: ~$0.0015, ~3.5s
- Cheapest (Call 3): $0.000139, 1.65s
- 21 calls total cost ~$0.03
